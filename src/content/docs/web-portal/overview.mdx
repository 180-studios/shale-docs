## Login

Enter your username and password to login to your account.

Your email address is your identity - when you are invited to a new organization and you use the same email address, it will be added to your list of teams, which are accessed by logging in at [The Shale App Landing Page](https://app.shalereview.com).

![Login screen](/images/screenshots/Shale%20App%20Login%201.png)

## Dashboard

The dashboard is your home page in Shale - here you will see all the latest project and note updates
and you can jump right in where you left off!

![Dashboard](/images/screenshots/Shale%20App%20Dashboard%201.png)

## Projects Page

The project page is where all of your projects live!

![Projects](/images/screenshots/Shale%20App%20Projects%201.png)

## Notes Page

The notes page shows all of your active and resolved notes and gives you a
quick glance at where to head next to streamline your creative work!

![Notes](/images/screenshots/Shale%20App%20Notes%201.png)
