<Aside>These tutorials are great for visual learners or anyone who prefers a guided walkthrough over reading docs.</Aside>

Vodie tutorials walk you through every major part of the Shale workflow — from account setup to giving and receiving feedback in 3D.

## What You'll Learn

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Getting Set Up" icon="setting">
    Create your account, set up an organization, and invite your first team members.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Uploading Content" icon="upload">
    Upload images, models, or renders via the browser or directly from your DCC plugin.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reviewing in 3D" icon="pencil">
    Navigate a scene, place spatial annotations, use the brush engine, and leave structured feedback.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Managing Approvals" icon="approve-check">
    Track feedback states, resolve notes, and push content through to final sign-off.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>
