Once the plugin is installed, you need to connect it to your Shale account.

## Steps

1. Open the **Shale panel** in the 3D Viewport sidebar (`N` key)
2. If you're not logged in, the panel will show **Offline** with a **Login** button
3. Click **Login** — a dialog will appear asking for your **Email** and **Password**
4. Enter your Shale credentials and click **OK**

The panel will connect to your account and show your available organisations.

## Staying Logged In

The plugin stays connected between sessions. If it shows Offline, check your internet connection and use the Login button to reconnect.
