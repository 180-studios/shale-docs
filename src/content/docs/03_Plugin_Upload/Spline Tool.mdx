The **ShaleSpline** places capture nodes along a curve through your scene. This is useful for corridors, walkways, or any environment where you want to guide reviewers along a specific path rather than covering a full volume.

## Adding a Spline

In the **Capture** tab, click **Add Spline**. A spline object will appear in your viewport. Edit its control points in Edit Mode to route the path through your scene — capture nodes are placed at the vertices of the spline.

## When to Use Spline vs Grid

- Use **Spline** when your space is linear — a hallway, a street, a walkthrough. The path guides the reviewer naturally from one end to the other.
- Use **Grid** when your space is open and you want reviewers to be able to stand anywhere within it, not just follow a route.

Like the Grid, the Spline is fully 3D — control points can be at different heights, making it suitable for staircases, ramps, or multi-level paths.

## Exporting

Export works the same as the Grid — click **Export Version** in the Capture tab to render and upload the environment to your linked Shale asset.
