Shale is a review and approval solution designed for real-time collaboration on 3D content across industries like game design, architecture, VFX, and construction.

It replaces disjointed feedback cycles with an integrated system for shared environment viewing, annotation, and iteration — connecting teams and decision-makers in a fluid, creativity-enabling review solution that removes hurdles, cuts down on needless calls, and creates faster iteration opportunities.

## Who is Shale for?

- **Game studios** — review levels, assets, and environments with your team and clients
- **Architecture firms** — walk through models and collect structured feedback from stakeholders
- **VFX teams** — annotate renders and 3D scenes with context-aware notes tied to camera view
- **Construction** — collaborate on BIM models and track approvals across project phases

## How it works

1. Connect Shale to your DCC tool (Unreal Engine, Blender, SketchUp, or Maya)
2. Create a review session and invite your team or share a timed guest link
3. Reviewers annotate directly on the 3D environment using the brush engine and comment tools
4. Feedback flows back into your DCC as embedded comment threads with approval states
5. Iterate, resolve, and track the full chain of custody through to final sign-off

## Platforms

Shale runs on **Web**, **Desktop**, and is optimized for **iPad and tablets**.
