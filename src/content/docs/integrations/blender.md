The Shale add-on for Blender integrates the review pipeline directly into your modeling and rendering workflow.

## Requirements

- Blender 3.6 or later
- Shale account with an active organization

## Installation

1. Download the Shale add-on (`.zip`) from the [Shale dashboard](https://shalereview.com)
2. In Blender, go to Edit → Preferences → Add-ons → Install
3. Select the downloaded `.zip` and enable the **Shale** add-on

## Starting a review

Open the Shale panel in the N-panel (press `N` in the viewport), sign in, and click **New Session**.
