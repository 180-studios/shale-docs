title: Unreal Engine
description: Using Shale with Unreal Engine.
---

The Shale plugin for Unreal Engine lets you launch review sessions directly from your UE project and receive annotated feedback as embedded comment threads.

## Requirements

- Unreal Engine 5.1 or later
- Shale account with an active organization

## Installation

1. Download the Shale plugin from the [Shale dashboard](https://shalereview.com)
2. Copy the plugin folder into your project's `Plugins/` directory
3. Open your project and enable **Shale** in Edit → Plugins
4. Restart the editor

## Starting a review

1. Open the Shale panel (Window → Shale)
2. Sign in with your Shale account
3. Click **New Session** to create a review environment from your current scene
4. Share the session link with reviewers

## Receiving feedback

Annotations and comments from reviewers appear in the Shale panel, tied to their original scene location and camera view. Click **Go To** on any note to jump directly to the reviewed position.
