The Shale extension for SketchUp brings collaborative review directly into your architectural modeling workflow.

## Requirements

- SketchUp Pro 2022 or later
- Shale account with an active organization

## Installation

1. Download the Shale extension (`.rbz`) from the [Shale dashboard](https://shalereview.com)
2. In SketchUp, go to Extensions → Extension Manager → Install Extension
3. Select the downloaded `.rbz` file

## Starting a review

Open the Shale toolbar, sign in, and click **New Session** to share your model for review.
